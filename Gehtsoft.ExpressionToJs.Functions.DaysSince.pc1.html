static public method Functions.DaysSince

Brief

Counts whole calendar days between two dates, ignoring the time of day.

Declaration
C#
double  DaysSince (DateTime args0, DateTime args1)

Parameters
args0

The reference (usually later) date; its time component is discarded.
args1

The baseline (usually earlier) date; its time component is discarded. The argument order sets the sign of the result: args0 after args1 gives a positive count, before it gives a negative count.

Details

Use it for "within N days" rules - expiry windows, cool-down periods, recent-activity checks - where only the calendar day matters and the time of day must not skew the count.
