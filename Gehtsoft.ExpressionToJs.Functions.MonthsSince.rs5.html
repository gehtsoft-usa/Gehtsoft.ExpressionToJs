static public method Functions.MonthsSince

Brief

Counts completed calendar months between two dates, ignoring partial months.

Declaration
C#
double  MonthsSince (DateTime args0, DateTime args1)

Parameters
args0

The reference (usually later) date; its time component is discarded.
args1

The baseline (usually earlier) date; its time component is discarded. As with Functions.DaysSince , the argument order sets the sign of the result.

Details

Use it for age-in-months or tenure rules where a partial month should not count - the result advances only when a full calendar month has elapsed, not on a raw 30-day division.
